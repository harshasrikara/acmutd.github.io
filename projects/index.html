ACM Projects

ACM Projects is a semester-long guided projects initiative for those new to software development, teaching collaborative tools, new technologies, and core development principles complimentary to curriculum

Previous Next

About

ACM Projects is designed to engage undergraduate students seeking to explore a collaborative software development environment. In this semester-long opportunity, these passionate and hard-working individuals come together in a team to create a unique, tangible app from scratch. Throughout the process, participants become familiar with modern tech stacks and tools used in the industry while guided by a project manager and an industry mentor. At the end of the ACM Projects experience, these students showcase their final product on presentation night.
