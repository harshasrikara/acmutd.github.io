What is ACM Education?

ACM Education was created to provide the additional academic and professional support needed for students to succeed outside of the classroom environment. The main goal for ACM Education is to offer programs andworkshops that allow students to build their technical skills and network with others to better prepare themselves for life after college.

Mentor Program

The ACM Mentor Program is a year-long program meant to help kickstart the journey of ECS underclassmen.

Interview Prep Workshops

A series of workshops we hold throughout the semester in hopes of preparing attendees for technical interviews and coding challenges.
